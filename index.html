Type of Game

Connect 4

How to play

Objective:

To be the first player to connect 4 of the same colored discs in a row (either vertically, horizontally, or diagonally)

How to play:

  • Players must alternate turns, and only one disc can be dropped in each turn.
  • On your turn, drop one of your colored discs into any of the columns.
  • You have to wait until your oponent finishes their turn before you can begin your own.
  • The game ends when there is a 4-in-a-row or a stalemate.
  • You can play as many rounds as you like.
  • Why don't you try playing best of 3 or 5 or even 7.
